## How to Use

### Adding a Pain Point
1. Increment the "Last Used ID" counter
2. Add entry to appropriate priority tier
3. Fill in all template fields
4. Update quick stats

### Weekly Review
1. Review each tier
2. Update frequency counts
3. Re-prioritize based on new evidence
4. Identify 2-3 focus areas
5. Create tasks for focus areas
6. Archive resolved items >30 days old

### Monthly Archive
1. Move resolved items older than 30 days to `archives/YYYY-MM-resolved.md`
2. Document resolution details and impact
